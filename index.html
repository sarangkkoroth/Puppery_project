About US

Founded in 2019,By sarang Centre for Puppetry and Performing Arts (CPPA) is a not-for-profit organisation dedicated to the promotion and preservation of puppetry, theatre and performing arts through research and performance.

CPPA provide a platform for puppeteers, performing artists, researchers and writers to work together within a multi-disciplined arena. As a resource, research and performing centre we encourage the development of new theatre in collaboration with artists and practitioners.

CPPA Publication

CPPA publishes works in Malayalam and English by writers in Kerala. We welcome new writing in the field of theatre, performance and associated disciplines, including drama, theatre/performance research papers, theatre reviews and essays. The work must not be previously published.

Our next publication is a selection of plays submitted for our play writing competition. For further details regarding submission, please refer to the News section of our website.
